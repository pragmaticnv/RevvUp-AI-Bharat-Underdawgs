# RevvUp - Requirements Document

## Team Information
- **Team Name:** Underdawgs
- **Hackathon:** AI for Bharat
- **Project Name:** RevvUp

## Problem Statement

The automotive modification industry in India faces significant challenges:

- **Fragmented Market:** Car enthusiasts struggle to find reliable information about compatible parts and modifications for their vehicles
- **Legal Uncertainty:** Lack of clarity around RTO compliance and Motor Vehicle Act regulations leads to illegal modifications and penalties
- **Visualization Gap:** Customers cannot preview how modifications will look on their specific vehicle before purchase
- **Trust Issues:** Difficulty in finding verified, trustworthy garages for professional installation
- **Information Asymmetry:** Limited access to comprehensive modification options and pricing transparency

## Proposed Solution

RevvUp is an AI-powered automotive modification platform that brings structure and transparency to India's car customization ecosystem. The platform combines cutting-edge 3D visualization, AI-driven compliance checking, and a verified service network to create a seamless modification experience.

## Key Features

### 1. 3D Virtual Garage
- Interactive web-based 3D configurator built with Three.js and React-Three-Fiber
- Real-time visualization of modifications on user's specific car model
- Support for multiple modification categories (body kits, wheels, spoilers, exhausts, etc.)
- 360-degree view with zoom and rotation capabilities
- Before/after comparison mode
- Save and share custom configurations

### 2. AI RTO Compliance Checker
- Powered by Amazon Bedrock (Claude v2) for intelligent legal analysis
- Real-time compliance verification against Indian Motor Vehicle Acts
- State-specific RTO regulation checking
- Detailed explanations of legal requirements and restrictions
- Modification approval probability assessment
- Documentation guidance for legal modifications

### 3. Verified Service Network
- Curated database of certified garages and modification shops
- Location-based garage recommendations
- User reviews and ratings system
- Direct booking and appointment scheduling
- Price quotes and estimates
- Installation warranty tracking

### 4. Smart Recommendations
- AI-powered part compatibility checking
- Personalized modification suggestions based on vehicle type
- Budget-aware recommendations
- Popular modification trends and inspiration gallery
- Community-driven modification showcases

## Target Users

- **Car Enthusiasts:** Individuals looking to customize their vehicles safely and legally
- **First-time Modifiers:** Users new to car modifications seeking guidance
- **Garage Owners:** Service providers looking to connect with customers
- **Legal Compliance Officers:** Users needing RTO compliance verification
